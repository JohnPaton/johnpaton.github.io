About me

Born in the UK, I lived as a child in England, Scotland, a German exclave in Switzerland, the USA, and, for most of my life, in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. I completed my bachelor's in Physics in Amsterdam, the Netherlands, and my master's in theoretical physics in Uppsala, Sweden, where I wrote my thesis on string theory. In November 2016 I began working as a data scientist & consultant at KPMG in the Netherlands, returning to live in Amsterdam.
